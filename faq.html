COMMON QUESTIONS

Answers To Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the questions our customers ask before visiting our store.

Do you provide wholesale pricing?

Yes. We offer competitive wholesale pricing for restaurants, caterers, offices, resellers and bulk buyers.

Do you sell products in retail?

Yes. Both retail and wholesale customers are welcome at our store.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, UPI and major digital payment methods.

Do you stock birthday decorations?

Yes. We stock balloons, banners, candles, party decorations and celebration essentials.

Can I place a bulk order in advance?

Absolutely. Contact us through phone or WhatsApp to discuss your requirements.

Do you have eco-friendly products?

Yes. We offer eco-friendly disposable alternatives including bagasse products and wooden cutlery.

Are all products available throughout the year?

Most products are available year-round, while seasonal items may vary depending on demand.

Can I contact you on WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is one of the quickest ways to enquire about products and pricing.
